Ways to Support

Our service projects are possible only with the community’s support. Your contribution enables us to accomplish Junior Auxiliary of Water Valley’s purpose to enrich the lives of children right here in Yalobusha County.

Join our Crown Club with a donation that supports every project the Junior Auxiliary of Water Valley leads. Every dollar supports direct services to children and families right here in Yalobusha County.
