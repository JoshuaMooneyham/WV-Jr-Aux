Scholarship Application

The Junior Auxiliary of Water Valley is pleased to offer four scholarships each spring, two $1,000 scholarships to be awarded to Water Valley Seniors and two $1,000 scholarships to Coffeeville High School Seniors based on scholastic merit, achievement in volunteering, future plans to volunteer, and letters of recommendation.
